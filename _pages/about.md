I'm Larry, a Forward Deployed AI scientist at [Boston Consulting Group](https://www.bcg.com/x/) based in the NYC office. I'm experienced in personalisation (uplift modelling, response modelling, model deployment) and GenAI (rag QA chatbot, pretraining). I was part of the DeepAI team where I built a personalization solution that delivered over 10M in projected incremental revenue and >20% uplift in upsell rates. I also open sourced a tabular ML starter repo used by DeepAI which allows projects to deliver impact quickly and at scale. Link [here](https://github.com/larrylawl/tabular-ml-boilerplate).

Previously, I was a team lead at [DSO National Laboratories](https://www.dso.org.sg) working on pretraining large language models (70B models on >200 GPUs). I'm most proud of leading the pretraining team (with no experience) to pretrain a model that's competitive with SEA AI Lab. There, I also contributed to the open-source pretraining library [TinyLlama](https://github.com/jzhang38/TinyLlama/issues/67).

I graduated from [National University of Singapore](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg) Computer Science with a first class honours. During my time there, I tried out research on NLP and bayesian optimization but found out I'm a better engineer than I am a researcher.
